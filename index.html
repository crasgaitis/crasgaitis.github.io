WORK EXPERIENCE


  • October 2022- present

    Undergraduate Researcher @ Orsborn Lab

    Code and debug a tablet-based "cursor tracking task/game" for rhesus macaque monkeys. Build supervised learning techniques to predict subjects' future performance on task and improve the design of training regiments. Helped analyze additional behavioral and neural data, focusing on motor learning and feedback & feedforward control. Occasionally supervised subjects interacting with tablet.


  • March 2023 - present

    Undergraduate Researcher @ Behavioral Ecophysics Lab

    Analyzed high-speed camera trap and GoPro footage of honeyeaters (birds) feeding at flowers, with the goal of determining whether honeyeaters were present in video frames, identifying honeyeater species, and counting the number of flowers probed per each bird's visit. Also worked on artificially augmenting datasets with randomized transformations and external data sources.


  • June 2023 - August 2023

    AI Intern @ NASA JPL

    Leveraged deep reinforcement learning methods to automate the scheduling of mission communications on the Deep Space Network. Missions are heavily constrained based on viewperiods, required equipment, antenna availability, "fairness" between competing missions, etc. with many special cases.
