    Music Mood • SWE Hacks • Apr 2023

    CorAL (Confused or Actually Learning) • NeuroTEC Hackathon • Feb 2023

    Built a dataset by having a subject wear an EEG device and watch videos in various languages, half of which the subject was fluent in. Then, trained a model to detect the subject's understanding, and connected it to an app. Using Arduino, a light turns on when the subject is confused. Could be used in classrooms, for teachers to check students' understanding based on neural feedback. Won Best in Technological Innovation.

    Pocket Plots • TreeHacks • Feb 2023

    I worked on the Pocket Plots' land recommendation system. Given a dataset of land plots for sale, I generated captions for the plots' images. When a user inputs the desired qualities of their ideal land plot (ex: flat terrain, nearby water, etc.), I rank the captions based on similarity to the user's description. Also added filters for square footage and price. Won Runner-Up for Best Startup by YC.

    Friend Flow• Hack Around the World 2 • Feb 2023
